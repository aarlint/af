Tracking the global impact of U.S. executive actions, 2025 – 2026

Data Sources

263 actions: 251 executive orders (EO 14147–14397) from the Federal Register, plus 12 military operations and Pentagon directives from official statements. Covers Jan 20, 2025 through Mar 2026.

Scoring Method

Each action is scored from each country's perspective on a 5-point scale:

+2 Strongly Positive +1 Positive 0 Neutral -1 Negative -2 Strongly Negative

Evaluation Process

Each country was evaluated independently by a dedicated AI analyst prompted to consider that nation's strategic interests, trade relationships, security concerns, and diplomatic priorities. Domestic US actions are scored 0 unless they have meaningful spillover effects.

Limitations

Scores are AI-generated assessments, not expert geopolitical analysis. They reflect likely directional impact, not magnitude. Second-order effects and long-term consequences may differ from initial scoring. All actions are weighted equally regardless of real-world significance.
